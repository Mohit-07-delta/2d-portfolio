Our Pillars of Open Knowledge

We organize workshops, editing drives, and collaborative events centered around the core projects of the Wikimedia movement.

Wikipedia Editing

Contributing accurate, sourced articles to the world's largest online encyclopedia. We focus on bridging content gaps and documenting local history and science.

  • Local History Archiving
  • Sourcing & Citations Workshops
  • Gender Gap Editing Campaigns

Wikimedia Commons

Uploading high-quality, free-to-use images, videos, and media files to enrich articles. We catalog our city's heritage and architectural wonders.

  • Vidisha Photo Walks
  • Creative Commons Licensing
  • Media Categorization

Wikidata & Open Data

Structuring open data so machines and humans can easily query and analyze it. We build databases that link Vidisha and SATI to global semantic nodes.

  • Semantic Data Queries
  • SPARQL Query Training
  • Academic Data Structuring

Digital Literacy

Fostering critical thinking, information evaluation, copyright awareness, and collaborative research tools among students at SATI.

  • Fact-Checking Workshops
  • Intellectual Property Education
  • Research Methodologies
