ABOUT THE CLUB

A student community for open knowledge.

Wiki Club SATI (South Asia Technical Initiative) is a community of students and volunteers who contribute to technical and other Wikimedia spaces. Initially started within an academic setting at Samrat Ashok Technological Institute, Vidisha, the community has expanded over the past two years to include members from multiple regions, promoting open knowledge and digital collaboration across the region.

Our Mission

To empower every SATI student to contribute meaningfully to Wikipedia, Wikimedia Commons, and Wikidata — building skills in research, writing, photography, and data literacy along the way.

Our Vision

A campus where every student has touched the world's largest free knowledge repository — and a Vidisha richly represented in the global commons.

WHAT WE DO

Club Activities

Promoting technical open knowledge projects, active collaboration, and community growth across the region.

Workshops

Hosting hands-on sessions to teach Wikipedia editing guidelines, formatting, citations, FOSS hardware, photography techniques, and audio editing to equip members with production skills.

Content Creation

Encouraging participants to write articles, catalog free-licensed photography, create audio files, and add reliable citations to guarantee article accuracy and content credibility.

Collaborative Projects

Organizing group initiatives where members work together to expand local stubs, translate pages, and improve Wikipedia articles around specific thematic research areas.

Edit-a-thons

Hosting timed sprint campaigns where editors collaborate to create and enrich articles, focusing on specific localized heritage, history, or scientific subjects.

Outreach & Promotion

Spreading awareness about the importance of open access information and free education by conducting presentations, talks, and campaigns inside and outside the campus.

Training & Resources

Developing and distributing step-by-step editing guides, tutorials, sandboxes, and educational templates to make the onboarding process smooth for new volunteers.

Strategic Partnerships

Collaborating with regional Wiki clubs, Wikimedia affiliates, open-source user groups, and educational organizations to exchange expertise, coordinate large-scale programs, and drive cross-community initiatives.

The Wikimedia Movement

The Wikimedia movement is a global community of volunteers who collectively build and maintain the largest free knowledge resources on the internet — Wikipedia, Wikimedia Commons, Wikidata, Wiktionary, and more.

Wiki Club SATI is part of this movement at the campus level — running training, hosting edit-a-thons, and mentoring new editors in their first contributions.
